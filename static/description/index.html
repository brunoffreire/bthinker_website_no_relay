Website No-Relay SMTP

Get e-mails from your website visitors!

When a visitor fills up a form on your website, the e-mail used as "FROM" will be the visitors email.

using this module, you will be abe to setup a mail tamplate that uses your SMPT server and on e-maill address from your own domain (defined by you on the mail template as well) so you can receive the messagens from the contact form of your website.

After installing, go to Definitions -> Technical -> Mail Templates.

  • Uncheck the "Base Templates" filter option and search for "website".
  • Edit and set the mail template as you need.
